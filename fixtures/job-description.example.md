# Staff Backend Engineer — acme-corp

We're looking for a Staff Backend Engineer to lead the next generation of our
distributed ingestion pipeline.

## What you'll do

- Design and own a low-latency ingestion path in **Go**, deployed on
  **Kubernetes** with full observability via **Prometheus** and
  **OpenTelemetry**.
- Evolve the **Kafka**-based event bus; optimize partitioning and
  retention.
- Drive CI/CD maturity: **GitHub Actions**, **Terraform**, reproducible
  builds, SBOMs, attestations.
- Mentor senior and mid-level engineers across 3 teams; run incident
  reviews with a blameless format.

## What we're looking for

- 10+ years in backend roles, at least 3 at the **Staff** level.
- Deep experience with **Go** or **Rust**; exposure to **TypeScript** is a
  plus.
- Strong fundamentals in **PostgreSQL**, **Redis**, and
  **Elasticsearch** or similar.
- Experience with **distributed systems**, **event-driven architectures**,
  and **gRPC**.
- Remote-first collaboration habits, async communication, clear writing.
- Security-aware: familiar with **SLOs**, **TLS**, secrets management.

## Nice to have

- Contributions to open source.
- Experience with **Nix** or reproducible-build tooling.
- Exposure to **Elixir** or functional programming.
